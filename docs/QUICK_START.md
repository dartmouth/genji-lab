This guide provides the fastest path to your first success with Genji. Follow the minimal steps to get started immediately.

## Step 2: Browse Document Collections

1. From the homepage, you'll see **Document Collections**
2. Collections organize related documents together (e.g., different translations of The Tale of Genji)
3. Click on a collection to view:
   - Collection metadata and description
   - All documents within the collection
4. âœ… Ready to explore documents!

## Step 3: Open and View Documents

1. Click on any document title within a collection
2. The document opens in a clean, focused reading interface
3. **Try the comparison feature:**
   - Open up to 3 documents side-by-side
   - Perfect for comparing different translations of the same passage
4. Navigate through document elements (chapters, sections, paragraphs)
5. âœ… Document viewing mastered!

## Step 4: Create Your First Annotation

1. **Select text** you want to annotate:
   - Click and drag your mouse over the text
   - The text will be selected
   
2. **Choose annotation type** from the context menu:
   - **Comments:** Basic notes and observations
   - **Scholarly Annotations:** Detailed academic analysis with tags
   - **External References:** Links to outside sources
   - **Linking Annotations:** Connect related passages across documents
   
3. **Write your annotation**:
   - Type your observation, question, or analysis
   - Keep it focused and specific
   
4. **Save**:
   - Click **"Save"**
   - âœ… Your first annotation is live!

## Step 5: View and Navigate Annotations

**See your annotation:**
- Annotated text is highlighted in different colors
- Click highlighted text to view its annotations
- Click "Show Annotations" at the bottom of the screen
- Hover over highlighted text to preview annotations
